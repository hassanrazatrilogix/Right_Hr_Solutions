Our Story

Founded by Marie M. Laplante, MBA, PHR, a seasoned Human Resources Executive with over 22 years of experience, Right HR Solutions was established to bring expert HR services to businesses of all sizes. Marie's mission has been clear from the start: to provide tailored HR solutions that foster positive workplace cultures, maintain compliance, and empower employees to excel. Today, our services encompass staffing, payroll management, training, and development—streamlining HR processes so that companies can focus on growth and success.

Marie’s background in HR spans strategic and generalist expertise across various sectors, making her a trusted partner for companies seeking effective HR solutions. Her commitment to quality and professionalism has earned Right HR Solutions multiple certifications, including:

  • Disadvantaged Business Enterprises & Airport Concessions (DBE-ACDBE)
  • Certified Women Owned Business Enterprise (CWOBE)
  • Office of Supplier Diversity (OSD)
  • Broward Health (BH)
  • Minority/Women Business Enterprise (M/WBE)
  • The School Board of Broward County (SBBC)
  • The School Board of Palm Beach County (SBPC)
  • Broward College (BC)
